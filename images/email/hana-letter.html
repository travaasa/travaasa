Aloha from Hana,

As you may know, in June 2011, Hotel Hana-Maui reopened under a new name—Travaasa Hana. We are proud to be the second property in a new collection of experiential hotels owned and operated by Travaasa Destinations.

At its core,Travaasa is about recapturing the magic of travel. The Hana you know and love will remain (we promise!), but we are very excited to now offer enriching activities that fall under five pillars central to our new brand—Adventure, Culinary, Culture, Fitness and Spa. We are passionate about continuing to rekindle the romance of old Hawai'i, and feel very fortunate to introduce programming that will serve to deepen that experience.

At Travaasa Hana, your journey is up to you to design—participate in traditional net fishing, horseback rides down the coastline, archery at the plantation house, yoga classes and more. There is something different each day and every guest has the opportunity to choose how much, or how little, to do.

We are also offering two different package pricing options: Total Travaasa™, an all-inclusive* program which includes meals, access to all on-site programming and a daily $125 resort credit that may be used towards spa services or select off-property activities and excursions; or Your Travaasa™, an á la carte stay. I would love to welcome you back; our recent guests have named us the #1 Readers' Choice in Hawaii on Condé Nast Traveler's Annual Gold List!

We look forward to being a part of your journey.

Mark Stebbings
General Manager
