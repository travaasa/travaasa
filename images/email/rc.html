POLICIES
•  Guests may access their rooms as early as 3:00pm on their check-in date and must be checked out of their room by 12 noon on their departure date. Late check-out, if available, will be at a nominal charge.
•  A one night deposit will be taken at the time of reservation. Cancellations more than 10 days prior to arrival date will receive a full refund. Within 10 days, the deposit will be charged.
•  Travaasa is reserved for guests 16 years and older. Guests under 21 must be accompanied by an adult.
•  ADA-compliant accommodations are available for each lodging option and meeting venue. Please contact us prior to your arrival.
•  Pets are not allowed on property. Service animals are always welcome.
Please be aware Travaasa is a non-smoking property and does not permit outside food or beverages in any common areas.
 
GREEN POLICY
•  Sustainable principles guided the design and building of Travaasa. We are committed to preserving the natural resources and beauty of the Texas Hill Country. Your cooperation in our ongoing efforts to conserve resources and minimize waste at Travaasa is appreciated.
•  Recycling receptacles are located throughout the property for your convenience.
•  Our housekeeping schedule is designed to minimize the use of water, detergent, and energy. Please contact the front desk if you require additional towels, toiletries, or other supplies.
