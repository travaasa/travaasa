May is for Moms at Travaasa

A stay at Travaasa Austin will provide a relaxing getaway for moms as well as time to reflect, revitalize and reconnect.

Throughout the month of May, Travaasa Austin will feature specially-designed programming focused on motherhood. Unique wellness programming will include a class to reshape the mommy midsection, pre- and post-natal yoga, and cooking demonstrations.

Unique offerings for the month of May include:

  • Reshape Your Motherly Midsection:"a core class focused on reshaping the abdominal muscles and pelvic floor, proper breathing and core activation to restore a healthy, toned shape
  • Cooking with Mom: a culinary demonstration that brings back memories of making cookies with mom or turning the crockpot on and letting it simmer for hours
  • Pre- and Post-Natal Yoga: guests modify poses and safely practice yoga during and after pregnancy with trained yoga instructors
  • Specially-designed meditation classes: helps guests focus on their relationship with their mother and how it effects their lives
  • A special edition of Travaasa Austin's Journaling class: guests jot down favorite stories and memories of mom
