From the desk of Mark Stebbings:

Aloha from Hana,

As you may know, in June 2011, Hotel Hana-Maui reopened under a new name—Travaasa Hana. We are proud to be the second property in a new collection of experiential hotels owned and operated by Travaasa Destinations.

At its core, Travaasa is about recapturing the magic of travel. The Hana you know and love will remain (we promise!), but we are very excited to now offer enriching activities that fall under five pillars central to our new brand—Adventure, Culinary, Culture, Fitness and Wellness. We are passionate about continuing to rekindle the romance of old Hawai'i, and feel very fortunate to introduce programming that will serve to deepen that experience.

I would love to welcome you back; for a limited time, guests who book a stay through March 31st can save up to 33% on our Rediscover the Magic of Hana Package. Our recent guests have named us the #1 Readers' Choice in Hawaii on Condé Nast Traveler's Annual Gold List!

We look forward to being a part of your journey.

Mark Stebbings
General Manager
