PRESIDENT'S DAY SOFTBALL TOURNAMENT – Hana, Maui
Hana’s traditional softball tournament takes place each year at Hana Ballpark, just a few steps from Travaasa Hotel Hana. The park was built by team owner Paul Fagan in the 1940s to hold the San Francisco Seals' summer camp. Mr. Fagan also began Travaasa Hotel Hana in 1946 and was the original owner of Hana Ranch. Join the community for cheering and lively entertainment throughout the tournament.
