VITAL ANTI-INFLAMMATORY WORKOUT – Austin
This is an interval training method that focuses on the recovery phase of the workout. We will perform an exercise as hard as we can for 1 minute, and then rest for as long as we need for our heart to recover, normally 2 minutes. The intensity of the exercises increases on each cycle and we will perform 3 to 5 cycles. This workout is intense challenging our bodies to its limits and creates a lot of awareness about how your heart pumps and how it recovers. The result is reduced inflammation due to the focus on the recovery phase of the workout. It increases our physical and mental ability to overcome fatigue over a period of time, increasing our vital capacities.

RADIANT CORE EXERCISES – Austin
This is an elegant system that develops integration of the core with the limbs. The exercises are meant to help with spinal and postural alignment that transfers to our daily activities. The focus is to connect the breathing with activation of the intrinsic muscles of the body, the inner unit, and working outward. The exercises are performed by positioning the body on a mat, in various ways to use gravity and body weight as resistance . Core exercises provide correct stabilization of the core, which allows freedom of movement and more power for the limbs. The ability to use breath and movement effectively will increase our stamina and vital forces, the side effect is a trimmer waistline.
